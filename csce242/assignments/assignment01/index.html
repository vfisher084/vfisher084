How to make Homemade Mac & Cheese!

Ingredients you'll need:

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Salt & pepper
  • Garlic powder

Getting started

There are lots of different ways to make mac and cheese. As a cheese enthusiast myself, I eat mac and cheese very often, usually in its instant or boxed form to save time. However, there's just something special about homemade mac and cheese that gives you all those warm and fuzzy feelings. It's just better, and fresher! The ingredients listed above are pretty much just your basic mac and cheese staples, so you can of course add whatever spices or extra toppings you'd like. The following recipe is one for baked mac and cheese that my mom has been making for years. Let's get started!

Preparation Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add in pasta of choice. Drain pasta and return to pot; add butter. Whisk together milk, evaporated milk, and eggs in a medium bowl. Add seasonings and salt and pepper to taste, and combine cheese in a separate bowl. Place the cooked and buttered pasta in the bottom of a baking dish, and sprinkle the cheese on top. Then pour the milk mixture over the pasta and cheese and bake 35 to 45 minutes, or until the top layer is lightly browned. Let stand before serving, then enjoy!

Ingredient Measurement
Pasta 3 cups
Cheese 24 oz
Milk 1/3 cup
Evaporated milk 2 cans (12 oz)
Butter (unsalted) 4 tbsp
Eggs 2 large
Garlic powder 1/4 tsp
